[caption id="attachment_460" align="aligncenter" width="614"] Gang of Grannies[/caption]

Tv: 1/80s; Av: F/4.5; f: 26mm; ISO: 200

Equipment: Nikon D3000 with standard 18-55 VR lens

Shot in NEF; processed with RAW Therapee

We came across these three old ladies sitting and chatting by the roadside near our resort in Punakha. They had a discussion among themselves on how to pose!

I like the fact that all three of them have varying expressions. It adds to the natural feel of the photo.

The largish gap between the lady on the right and the on in the middle kind of spoils the snap though. It would have been so much better if I had got them to sit closer together!
