Tv: 1/100s; Av: F/7.1; f: 28mm; ISO: 200

Equipment: Nikon D3000 with standard 18-55 VR lens

Shot in NEF, processed with RAW Therapee

I know what you're thinking: what's the point of this photo? I have no idea :-)

There was some beautification work going on at our resort in Chamkar in Bumthang valley. They were laying these stones for .. I don't know .. some kind of walkway or something. The placement and the smooth stones caught my attention and I thought I'd experiment with them.

As so often happens with photography - I can't quite place my finger on what I like about this photo - but I do like it! Critique welcome.
