By making the background B&W, it gives the photo a sense that it is an old picture and reminds me of my childhood days when we used to go to the farm and eat the fruit.

Further Improvements:

Care should be taken while selecting the fruit, meaning here in this picture even though the color of the fruit is brilliant and rich, the fruit looks is a little damaged and infected. A uninfected fruit would have been more pleasing.
